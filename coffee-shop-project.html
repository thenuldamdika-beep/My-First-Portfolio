Artisanal Coffee Landing Page Design

Role UI/UX Designer
Timeline 1 Week
Tools Figma, Photoshop
Category Branding / E-commerce

Project Overview

I designed a highly creative, aesthetic coffee shop brand landing page from scratch. The primary goal was to craft an inviting, premium digital experience that perfectly captures the warm ambiance of an artisanal cafe.

The UI relies on a carefully selected mocha color palette, modern typography, and a very clean fluid layout to invite users in and establish a high-end online identity.
